Stopwatch - Online

Online stopwatch

It is used to easily measure a lapse of time; this timer has a pause / resume function and time backup.

Start the stopwatch online via the "start" button or the space bar on your keyboard, the space bar also allows you to stop or restart the timer.

Help

Keyboard Shortcuts for free stopwatch

Key

  • Spacebar: Use the spacebar to start and stop the stopwatch

Buttons

  • "Start" starts the clock for the first time,
  • "Stop" stops the clock,
  • "Start 0" starting from 0 while maintaining backups,
  • "Resume" resumes timer starting time has already elapsed,
  • "Reset": Deletes all results and reset to 0 the stopwatch.

Some technical details

  • Display in the tab to remain visible whilst navigating
  • Activation of the timer with the space bar on your keyboard or your mouse
  • Able to export using "copy / paste" to a spreadsheet
  • Your timers remain active on the website even if you close your browser
