Timer or hourglass

A timer is a specialized type of clock for measuring time intervals. A timer a device which counts down from a specified time interval is more usually called a timer or a hourglass.

Some technical details

  • Display in the tab as so to remain visible whilst navigating
  • Colorful display in green, orange and red for less than a minute, 30 seconds and 15 seconds respectively
  • Ringing alarm
  • Elapsed time display if exceeded
  • Activation countdown timer with a single mouse click for more than 30 pre-programmed times
  • Remove timer in one click.
